Vision and Mission of Mechanical Department
Vision:

Value addition in students by providing a interactive working environment where they are free to create, innovate and interact with Science, Technology and Management for the sustainable development of the society.

Mission:
  • To achieve a competitive edge in the areas of Mechanical, Manufacturing, Automation and Management through continuous learning, upcoming technologies and high-quality research.
  • To make innovators and entrepreneurs to design and develop new products and services to address social, technical and business challenges.
  • To develop the skills and prepare students as a workforce where success is achieved not just from what one knows, but what one is able to do with that knowledge.
  • To provide an excellent opportunity to interact with industries and develop a practical perspective of the holistic learning.
Program Educational Objectives:

The four year courses aim to achieve the following PEOs.

  • Provide students with a strong foundation in the fundamentals of Mathematics, Science and Engineering to enable them to devise and deliver efficient solutions to challenging problems in the areas of Manufacturing, Automation and Management.
  • Be receptive to new technologies and attain professional competence through lifelong learning such as advanced degrees, professional registration, publications and other professional activities.
  • Practice professional ethics consistent with a sense of social responsibility so that they can work as independent individuals as well as a member of the multi-disciplinary teams.
  • Develop the ability to design and conduct experiments, as well as to organize, analyze and interpret data to produce meaningful conclusions and recommendations to the society.

Further, the faculty members are open to new and challenging domains with consistent efforts to provide solutions.
